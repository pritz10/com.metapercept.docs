Style Concept Topics

This task explains how to format the concept topics for a customized WebHelp output.
  1. Open a DITAxPresso project.
  2. Click Styler > WebHelp > Header.
  3. Click HEADER. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
  4. Click CHAPTER CONTENT. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
  5. Click LEADER. Choose suitable options for the fields you want to customize.
  6. Click Save.
